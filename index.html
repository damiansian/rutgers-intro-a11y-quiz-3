About Our Alliance

The Horizon Student Alliance was founded in 2018 by a group of students who believed campus life could be more inclusive, connected, and fun. Since then, we've grown to over 400 active members.

What We Offer

– Weekly community meetups
– Mentorship matching program
– Annual leadership retreat
– Campus advocacy campaigns
– Free professional headshot days
